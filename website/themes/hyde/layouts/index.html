Biography

Sam Ginzburg is a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Princeton University. He is a part of the SNS Research Group, and is advised by Michael Freedman. He is primarily working on finding new ways to improve cloud serverless (FaaS) platforms.


He previously worked in the PLASMA Research Group at the University of Massachusetts - Amherst, with Emery Berger, as well as the UConn Plant Computational Genomic Lab with Jill Wegrzyn.
